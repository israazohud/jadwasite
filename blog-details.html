Jadwa sign an agreement to absorb training and coexistence.

Funded by the United Nations Development Program (UNDP) and as part of the Al Fakhoora Scholarships and Empowerment Program, Jadwa Economic Consulting and Business Solutions and the Development Action Center signed an agreement together to spend a training and living period for accounting graduates. It is noteworthy that Jadwa has a cloud-based software structure through which it provides its services in accounting, auditing and automated management of small and emerging projects; All of the above is within the framework of the Jadwa Oasis Program, which includes a platform for action and capacity building.
